Our Services

It can be hard to visualise your design ideas, see the space you have to work with.

Using 3D drawings and visualisation, the vital details of your plan can be highlighted and corrected before it even becomes a problem and an extra expense to correct.

3D tools can help you to make sure all details have been considered from the direction of the vital elements of the space such as: doors, windows, heating elements.

Making sure your space is functionally and aesthetically well designed.

Residential Design

Our mission is to create or transform residential properties across Ireland, from start to finish, at an affordable cost. We can help home builders and owners get the most out of their homes with new builds, extensions, renovations or energy upgrades. We work in close partnership with our clients to explore ideas and solutions to ensure our designs are tailored to meet their needs, property and budget.

Office Design

CUIB provides Interior office solutions for commercial spaces. From the start our goal was to manufacture and supply products to the market from local Irish sources and we have now developed our own range of meeting pods and acoustic solutions. We work closely with Architects, Designers and Clients taking a brief from concept to completion covering all aspects of Internal Commercial office Space.

Commercial Design

We have considerable experience in the design and project management of large commercial projects across Ireland. This has included projects for use in health care, general office, retail (including garden centre), residential multi-storey apartment blocks, housing developments, hospitality, leisure, education, entertainment and industrial. Our most recent project includes the current development of a new bar and restaurant project in Cork and a gym in Limerick.
